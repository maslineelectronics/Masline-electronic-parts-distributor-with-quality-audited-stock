## Multi-Layer Quality Inspection Process

To maintain high reliability, Masline follows a multi-step quality control workflow:

1. **Visual and Microscopic Inspection**  
   - Identifies surface anomalies, scratches, tampering, or resealing signs.

2. **Electrical Testing & Functionality Verification**  
   - Confirms that ICs, sensors, passives, and power components meet performance specifications.

3. **X-ray & Structural Analysis (when applicable)**  
   - Ensures internal die and bonding structures match original manufacturer designs.

4. **Marking and Label Verification**  
   - Cross-checks manufacturer logos, batch codes, date codes, and traceability.

5. **RoHS & Compliance Validation**  
   - Ensures adherence to global environmental standards.

6. **Packaging & ESD-Handling Confirmation**  
   - Guarantees components are stored and shipped in optimal conditions.

These steps ensure every part is thoroughly reviewed before it reaches the customer.
